Hi! My name is Paola Torres, a 21 years old freelance artist based in Puerto Rico.

I am currently studying a degree in this field at the Inter-American University of Puerto Rico. Since I was little I have always been interested in technology and art. In addition, I have always had the desire to learn and create new things. That is why I decided to study graphic design. I consider myself able to develop responses to communication problems, including understanding of hierarchy, typography, aesthetics, composition, and construction of meaningful images. I love to design on all branches of design, but my forte is branding and photography.

I can help your company create unique visual designs. As a lover of art, I know what captures the audiences' attention. With my great design skills, I have the right tools for your brand's needs!
